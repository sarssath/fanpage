Born on September 10, 1988, Sheck Wes (given name Khadimoul Rassoul Cheikh Fall), is a Harlem and Milaukee raised Sengalese rapper, songwriter, and model. He is most known for his 2017 track "Mo Bamba" which went extremely viral in 2018, named after his close friend and basketball player, Mo Bamba. He is jointly signed to both Kanye West's GOOD Music and Travis Scott's Huncho Jack label. He recently released his debut project, Mudboy on October 5, 2018.
